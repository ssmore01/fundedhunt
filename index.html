📐 How We Calculate Trust Scores

Each firm is independently scored out of 100 using 8 criteria developed by Funded Hunt. Our Trust Radar helps traders avoid scam firms and find the most reliable prop firm challenge for your trading style. Scores are not influenced by affiliate commission rates, firm advertising, or promotional arrangements.

⏳ Firm Age & Track Record
Older firms with more operational history score higher. Firms under 1 year are penalised.
💸 Verified Payout Evidence
Publicly documented payouts — screenshots, social proof, Trustpilot mentions — are weighted heavily.
📋 Rule Transparency
Firms with clearly published, stable rules score higher than those with vague or frequently changed terms.
🌐 Community Size & Sentiment
Discord/social community size and Trustpilot sentiment — weighted by review volume, not raw star rating.
🏛️ Regulatory & Legal Clarity
Firms disclosing jurisdiction, legal entity, and registered address score higher than anonymous or offshore-only firms.
🔁 Payout Consistency
Firms with no public dispute history or delayed payout complaints score higher. Recent payout issues lower the score.
📈 Total Capital Distributed
Firms with independently verified total payout figures score higher than those with unverifiable claims.
🤝 Trader-Friendliness
Fee refund policies, flexible rules, scaling plans, and responsive support are factored into the final score.
Score Bands:  🟢 85–100 = High Trust  |  🟡 70–84 = Moderate Trust  |  🔴 Below 70 = Caution
